## Product Segmentation
Standard vs. Professional Nose Clips

The market is segmented into standard and professional freediving nose clips. Standard models are lightweight, flexible, and suitable for recreational divers and beginners. Professional models are designed for competitive divers, offering enhanced grip, reduced water leakage, and materials resistant to saltwater corrosion, providing reliability during extended dives and training sessions.

Silicone, Rubber, and Thermoplastic Materials

Material type is another important segmentation factor. Silicone nose clips dominate the market due to their flexibility, comfort, and resistance to water-induced wear. Rubber models are often used for cost-effective solutions, while thermoplastic options offer durability and better performance for frequent use. The choice of material directly influences comfort, longevity, and price, affecting consumer preferences in various regions.
